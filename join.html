Join the Lab

AIMLab (Artificial Intelligence in Medicine Lab) welcomes motivated students and researchers who are passionate about building reliable AI for healthcare. Our research spans AI-aided diagnosis systems, medical image processing and analysis, computer vision applications, and biomedical signal modeling, with an emphasis on practical impact in smart healthcare and home-care.

AI-assisted Diagnosis Medical Imaging Computer Vision Biomedical Signals Smart Healthcare & Home Care

Who Should Apply

We value curiosity, strong fundamentals, and engineering discipline. Applicants from Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Biomedical Engineering, or related fields are welcome.

Prospective PhD Students

Prospective PhD applicants interested in medical AI are encouraged to apply through the NYCU Institute of Intelligent Bioelectrical Engineering (IBE) graduate portal. In your statement of purpose, please describe your research interests and mention your interest in joining AIMLab and working with Prof. Chia-Yen Lee.

Master Students

We welcome motivated master’s students who want to build solid research skills in machine learning / deep learning, medical imaging, and AI-aided diagnosis systems. Prior experience in biomedical signals or computer vision is helpful, but not required.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome applications from postdoctoral researchers with experience in machine learning, medical image analysis, computer vision, and/or biomedical signal processing. To apply, please email your CV and a short research statement.

What We Look For

  • Solid programming skills in Python; experience with PyTorch/TensorFlow is a plus.
  • Ability to read papers, design experiments, and write clear documentation.
  • Commitment to reproducible research (version control, clean code, and transparent reporting).
  • Experience in medical imaging / computer vision / biomedical signals is beneficial but not mandatory.

Collaboration

We are open to collaborations with academic teams, medical institutions, and industry partners interested in AI-driven healthcare technologies.
